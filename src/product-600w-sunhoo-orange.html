The Sunhoo 600W Portable UPS Power Station has an array of powerful features designed to make your life easier during load-shedding and power outages:
Impressive Power Output – With a 600W Pure Sine Wave Inverter that can be boosted to handle up to 1000W, this device can power a wide range of electronics and appliances.
High-Performance Battery – Equipped with a 672Wh Lithium-Iron Phosphate Battery (LiFePO4) offering approximately 2000 battery cycles, ensuring durability and reliability. After 2000 cycles 80 – 85% battery capacity still remains.
Rapid Recharging – The AC Max Fast Charge feature can recharge the battery from 0 to 100% in just 90 minutes, ensuring you’re not left in the dark for long.
Smart Drive Inverter – Automatically activates when power consumption exceeds 600W, ensuring uninterrupted power supply for your devices.
Emergency Backup: Features built-in UPS functionality, guaranteeing seamless power backup during emergencies.
User-Friendly App: Stay connected with the built-in Wi-Fi and user-friendly iOS and Android app, enabling remote monitoring and control from your smartphone.
Advanced Battery Management – Your safety is a priority with built-in protections against over-charging, over-discharging, and over-temperature issues.
Real-Time Monitoring – The intuitive Smart LED display provides real-time updates on power usage and battery life, making it easy to keep track of your device’s status.
Versatile Charging Options – Enjoy flexibility with three recharging methods: solar, AC, and DC.
